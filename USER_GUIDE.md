# User Guide — AI Playlist Generator

## Overview

The AI Playlist Generator lets you create Spotify playlists by describing a mood, genre, or vibe in plain English. The app uses AI to recommend songs, verifies they exist on Spotify, and saves the result as a new playlist on your Spotify account.

---

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Log in

1. Open the app in your browser.
2. Click **Login with Spotify** on the landing page.
3. Authorize the app when prompted by Spotify.
4. You will be redirected back to the app and logged in.

> Your session stays active for 24 hours. After that, you will need to log in again.

---

## Creating a Playlist

### Step 1: Describe your playlist

In the text box, type a description of the playlist you want. For example:

- "upbeat 80s workout bangers"
- "chill lo-fi study vibes"
- "90s alternative rock anthems"
- "late night jazz café"

The more specific you are, the better the results.

### Step 2: Choose the number of songs

Use the **song count slider** to select how many tracks you want (10 to 30, in increments of 5).

### Step 3: Generate

Click **Generate Playlist**. The app will:

1. Send your description to the AI for song recommendations.
2. Verify each recommended song exists on Spotify.
3. Show you a preview of the selected tracks.

You will see a loading spinner while this processes. It typically takes a few seconds.

---

## Reviewing and Saving

### Preview screen

After generation, you will see:

- A **numbered list** of the AI-selected songs.
- A **playlist name** field (prefixed with "AI Mix:" — you can edit it).

From here you have three options:

| Button | Action |
|--------|--------|
| **Save to Spotify** | Creates the playlist on your Spotify account and opens it. |
| **Regenerate** | Re-runs the AI with the same description to get different song picks. |
| **Start over** | Returns to the main screen to create a new playlist. |

### Success screen

After saving, you will see:

- The playlist name
- The number of tracks
- A link to **Open in Spotify**

You can click **Make another** to return to the main screen and create another playlist.

---

## Error Handling

If something goes wrong (network error, Spotify API issue, etc.), an error card will appear with a message describing the problem. Click **Try again** to return to the main screen and attempt again.

---

## Logging Out

Click **Logout** to clear your session. You will be returned to the landing page.

---

## Tips for Better Results

- Be specific about era, genre, mood, or activity.
- Mention artists or sounds as reference points.
- Try combining multiple descriptors (e.g., "melancholy indie folk for rainy days").
- If the results aren't quite right, try **Regenerate** — the AI may pick different songs with the same prompt.
